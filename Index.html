About AffiliatePilot

AffiliatePilot is a next-generation, AI-driven platform founded by Hamdan with a single mission: to help online shoppers worldwide save money on every purchase. In an era of dynamic pricing, flash sales, and cross-border e-commerce, it's harder than ever to know if you're getting the best deal. That's where we step in.

Our proprietary AI assistant, PilotBot, operates 24/7 to monitor prices across major global platforms like Amazon, eBay, and AliExpress. It analyzes pricing trends, alerts users the moment prices drop, and automatically curates the best daily deals. But we don't stop at price tracking. AffiliatePilot provides real-time currency conversion, international shipping cost estimation, and intelligent product comparisons to ensure you know the true "landing cost" of any item.

Hamdan personally tests and curates tech, home, and lifestyle products to ensure our community of over 50,000 global shoppers gets only the most reliable recommendations. We believe in 100% transparency, which is why we openly disclose our affiliate partnerships. Our tools are free to use, with an optional Pro tier for users who need instant alerts and ad-free experiences.
